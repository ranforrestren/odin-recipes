Spanakopita

Description

Spanakopita, a delicious savory Greek pie made of crispy phyllo dough and filled with spinach and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • Chopped Spinach
  • Feta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Dill Weed
  • Parsley
  • Onions
  • Phyllo Dough

Steps

  1. Mix filling ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Line baking sheet with phyllo dough.
  3. Evenly spread filling into sheet, then layer with more phyllo dough.
  4. Bake in 325o oven for 1 hour, until crispy and golden brown.

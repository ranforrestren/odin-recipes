Lamb Kebabs

Description

Tender and juicy Greek Lamb Kebabs, perfect for grilling and serving alongside Tzatziki sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground Lamb
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Sumac
  • Chili Flakes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Form kebabs over wooden skewers.
  3. Brush grill with olive oil, then grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Serve topped with Tzatziki sauce.

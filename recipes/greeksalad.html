Greek Salad

Description

Tasty nutritious Greek Salad made from Feta cheese, Olives, Cucumbers, Onions, Green Peppers, Tomatoes, Olive Oil, and Vinegar.

Ingredients

  • Feta Cheese
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Cucumbers
  • Onions
  • Green Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Dice feta cheese, cucumbers, onions, peppers, and tomatoes.
  2. Combine olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Combine all ingredients, drizzle with vinegraette and toss.
  4. Add grinded black peppers and crumbled feta to taste.
